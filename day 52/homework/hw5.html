Harry Potter

Introduction

Harry Potter is a fictional character and the protagonist of J.K. Rowling's "Harry Potter" series. He is a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday.

Hogwarts

Harry attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he was sorted into Gryffindor House. He learned magic and formed lifelong friendships with Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley.

Adventures

Throughout the series, Harry battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, uncovers secrets about his family, and proves himself as "The Boy Who Lived."
